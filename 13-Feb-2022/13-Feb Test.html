LATA MANGESHKAR (1929-2022)

A Short Biography of Lata Mangeshkar

  • Lata Mangeshkar was born on 28 September 1929 in the city of Indore, MP, India.
  • Her real name was Hema Mangeshkar.
  • She is widely considered to have been one of the greatest and most influential singers in India.
  • She was known as the Nightingale of India, Voice of the Millennium and Queen of Melody.
  • Mangeshkar recorded songs in over thirty-six Indian languages and a few foreign languages.
  • She also sang in English, Russian, Dutch, and even Swahili.
  • In 1989 the Dadasaheb Phalke Award was bestowed on her by the Government of India.
  • She is only the second female singer, after M. S. Subbulakshmi, to receive this honour.
  • Lata Ji sang a patriotic song against the backdrop of the Sino-Indian War in January 1963.
  • The song was "Aye Mere Watan Ke Logo" in the presence of Jawaharlal Nehru, then the Prime Minister of India.
  • Lata Mangeshkar passed away on february 06,2022 in Mumbai, India
